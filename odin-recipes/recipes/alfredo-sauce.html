Alfredo Sauce

Description

This Alfredo sauce recipe always turns out rich and creamy!
A delicious way to transform plain fettuccine pasta or steamed vegetables.

Ingredients

  • butter
  • heavy cream
  • Parmesan cheese
  • garlic
  • fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over low heat. Stir in cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Add cheese and garlic; whisk continuously until heated through. Stir in parsley and serve.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!
